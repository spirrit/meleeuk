changelog

April 10 2022

Big update today. Firstly, we changed over to netlify for hosting, which should make things a little more convenient. I changed a lot of elements to more semantically appropriate ones, and added a ton of accessability improvements (big thanks to brik for the help).

I also added some buttons at the bottom of the page. I like the concept but i'm unsure whether I want it to remain a permanent feature of the site. I need to rebuild the links page or perhaps do away with it, this remains to be seen.

April 8 2022

I have changed and added a great deal. Layout revamped, js optimised, visuals adjusted. I'm slowly inching toward a melee.uk that I am proud of. There is much to do...

I like the somewhat minimal, angular look the top bar has right now. something about it really stokes joy. Who knows what it will look like in a few days.

April 6 2022

Cleaned up a lot of the site, added the ranking page with a detailed PR table. Please let me know if I got anything wrong!

April 5 2022

I've found some time to work on this website, so expect a trickle of updates as I fix everything and add much needed information and functionality like a pretty PR table and other stuff
